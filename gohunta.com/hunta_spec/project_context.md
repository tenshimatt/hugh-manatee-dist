# Project Context – Hunta

Hunta is a web platform built for people who use dogs for hunting, tracking, and field work. This includes upland bird hunters, retrievers, boar dog handlers, and trackers who work in rural and wilderness settings. They demand reliability, performance, and clarity — both in their dogs and in their tools.

Key needs:
- Offline-capable trail and training logs
- Trusted community with real-world experience
- Gear insights and ethical hunting standards

Hunta is built with these users at the center of every design decision.
