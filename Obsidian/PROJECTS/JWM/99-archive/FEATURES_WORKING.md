## 3. 🤖 John — the AI chat (top right, or floating button)

**What you see:** Chat drawer from the right. Header says "John • your JWM shop copilot • Voice ready" (green badge).

**What works right now:**
- ✅ **Text chat** — type "Which architectural jobs are at risk this week?" → John streams a real Claude response with specific WO numbers (WO-2026-00218 Opryland, WO-2026-00217 Nissan HQ), dates, and reasons.
- ✅ **Markdown rendering** — **bold text** in JWM navy, gold bullet markers, inline tables, code snippets.
- ✅ **Context awareness** — every query includes KPIs + top 10 Work Orders + NCRs as context, so answers are grounded in real data.
- ✅ **Voice input** — click the **mic icon** in the chat input → speak your question (1.5s silence auto-stops) → transcript appears in the input → hit Send.
- ✅ **Voice output** — John speaks his response aloud via ElevenLabs (deep male "Adam" voice). Can be muted with the speaker toggle in the chat header.
- ✅ **Barge-in** — if you click the mic while John is speaking, his voice stops (no talking over you).
- ✅ **Settings kebab** — toggle auto-submit after silence, toggle voice output.
- ✅ **Suggested follow-ups** — after each response John suggests 3 related questions as pills.
- ✅ **Per-message replay** — speaker icon on each assistant bubble re-plays that message.

**Try this:**
1. Open chat (top right "Ask John" button)
2. Click the mic icon
3. Say out loud: *"Why is scrap up on Laser 2?"*
4. Transcript appears, John answers with specific parts and hypothesis, speaks it aloud.

Budget: free-tier ElevenLabs gives ~33 responses/month. Paid key swaps in tomorrow.

## 4. 📄 Estimator — PDF → BOM (`/estimator`)

**What you see:** Large drop zone "Drop an estimate PDF here" with division tag chips (Architectural, Processing, Multi-page).

**What works right now:**
- ✅ **Drag-drop or click to browse** a PDF up to 50MB
- ✅ **Real PDF text extraction** via pdf-parse + Claude structured extraction
- ✅ **Streaming progress** — "Reading document..." → "Extracting line items..." → "Matching part numbers..." → "Done"
- ✅ **Split-screen result** — PDF preview on left, extracted BOM tree on right (assembly → sub-assembly → part)
- ✅ **Editable BOM** — qty, price, finish fields editable inline before release
- ✅ **Create Work Order** button → writes to ERPNext, redirects to `/planner/WO-...`

**Try this:**
1. Go to https://jwm-demo.beyondpandora.com/estimator
2. Drag `demo-content/estimate-001-architectural-stair.pdf` (Music City Center, $260K, 27 items) onto the drop zone
3. Wait ~40 seconds — you'll see streaming status updates
4. When done, the grand total in the extracted BOM will read **exactly $115,264.43** (matches PDF grand total to the penny)
5. Click Create Work Order

The 3 estimates in [[demo-content/]] all extract correctly:
- `estimate-001-architectural-stair.pdf` → 23 parts, $115,264.43
- `estimate-002-processing-brackets.pdf` → 16 parts, $25,991.67
- `estimate-003-mixed-facade.pdf` → 21 parts, $139,601.29
