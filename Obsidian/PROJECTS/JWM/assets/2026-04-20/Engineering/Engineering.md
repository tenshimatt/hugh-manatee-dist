We have two disciplines within engineering (and its really drafting and programming more than engineering)

One is the ACM side – (only used for Arch)

                We get field dimensions or scans from the field. Apply the dimensions to our shop drawings, this is the Layout Phase. (LO)

                Once layout is approved, we sketch each individual panel. Ie there could be 20 of 1 type, or 1 of 1 type, but there is a sketch for each type.

Once these sketches are approved. It is moved to programming.

Programming programs the sketches onto the machines. CNC’s AXYZ.

And its released to the Shop.

The other is our Plate and tube side (used for both)

                Parts are drawn, redrawn etc. as needed.

                Programmed for the machines

                Released to the shop.
