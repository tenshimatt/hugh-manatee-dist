## Hunter Psychology Profiles

Based on research, hunters fall into four primary behavioral categories:

- **Achievement-Oriented (35%)**: Performance-focused, data-driven
- **Community-Focused (30%)**: Social connection and knowledge sharing
- **Tradition-Preserving (25%)**: Heritage and values-based
- **Innovation-Seeking (10%)**: Technology adoption and optimization
