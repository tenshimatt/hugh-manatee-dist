## Hunter Psychology Profiles

The system recognizes four primary hunter personality types:

### 1. Achievement-Oriented Hunters (35%)
- **Characteristics**: Performance-focused, data-driven, goal-oriented
- **Motivations**: Progress tracking, competition, skill improvement
- **Engagement Strategy**: Metrics dashboards, achievement systems, leaderboards
- **Behavioral Adaptations**: Aggressive progress visualization, competitive elements

### 2. Community-Focused Hunters (30%)
- **Characteristics**: Social connection seekers, knowledge sharers, mentors
- **Motivations**: Helping others, social recognition, relationship building
- **Engagement Strategy**: Community features, mentorship programs, social proof
- **Behavioral Adaptations**: Social sharing tools, group activities, recognition systems

### 3. Tradition-Preserving Hunters (25%)
- **Characteristics**: Heritage-focused, values-driven, wisdom sharers
- **Motivations**: Preserving traditions, passing on knowledge, respect
- **Engagement Strategy**: Heritage content, mentorship roles, legacy features
- **Behavioral Adaptations**: Traditional framing, respect-based rewards, wisdom sharing

### 4. Innovation-Seeking Hunters (10%)
- **Characteristics**: Early adopters, technology enthusiasts, optimizers
- **Motivations**: Efficiency, new techniques, problem-solving
- **Engagement Strategy**: Advanced features, customization, beta programs
- **Behavioral Adaptations**: Cutting-edge tools, experimentation opportunities
