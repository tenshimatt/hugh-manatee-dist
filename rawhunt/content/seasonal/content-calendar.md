# GoHunta Seasonal Content Calendar & Messaging Templates

## Annual Content Strategy Overview

The hunting community operates on a natural seasonal rhythm that drives engagement, training activities, and content consumption. Our content calendar aligns with this rhythm while providing consistent value throughout the year.

### Seasonal Engagement Patterns
- **Spring (March-May):** High training engagement, preparation planning
- **Summer (June-August):** Peak training activity, equipment preparation
- **Fall (September-November):** Maximum platform engagement during hunting season
- **Winter (December-February):** Reflection, planning, and skill development
