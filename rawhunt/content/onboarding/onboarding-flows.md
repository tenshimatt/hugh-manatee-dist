"Your account is ready, and we're excited to help you track hunts, train your pack, and connect with fellow hunters who share your passion for the outdoors.

Let's start by setting up your hunting profile so we can provide personalized recommendations and connect you with relevant community discussions."

## Experience Level-Specific Onboarding

### New Hunter Onboarding Path
**Welcome Message:**
"Welcome to hunting! You're starting an incredible journey, and the GoHunta community is here to support you every step of the way."

**Personalized Recommendations:**
- Suggested first content: "Hunting Safety Fundamentals"
- Community introduction: "New Hunter Support Group"
- Feature focus: Basic hunt logging and safety resources
- Mentor connection: "Find an Experienced Hunter Mentor"

**First Week Goals:**
1. Complete hunter safety resource review
2. Log your first hunt (even if just scouting)
3. Introduce yourself to the community
4. Connect with a mentor hunter

**Encouraging Messages:**
"Every expert was once a beginner. Take your time, focus on safety, and don't hesitate to ask questions."

### Developing Hunter Onboarding Path
**Welcome Message:**
"Great to have another developing hunter in our community! You're at an exciting stage where consistent practice leads to significant improvement."

**Personalized Recommendations:**
- Suggested content: "Intermediate Hunting Strategies"
- Community groups: "Skill Development Forum"
- Feature focus: Pattern analysis and training management
- Expert connections: "Advanced Technique Discussions"

**First Week Goals:**
1. Log your last three hunts to establish baseline data
2. Set up training schedules if you have dogs
3. Join relevant skill-building discussions
4. Identify areas for improvement focus

**Motivational Message:**
"This is where hunting gets really interesting - you know the basics and you're ready to refine your skills for better results."

### Experienced Hunter Onboarding Path
**Welcome Message:**
"Welcome! Your experience is valuable to our community, and we're excited to help you optimize your already successful hunting."

**Personalized Recommendations:**
- Suggested content: "Advanced Analytics and Pattern Recognition"
- Community role: "Consider sharing expertise with developing hunters"
- Feature focus: Advanced data analysis and community contribution
- Expert network: "Professional Hunter Network"

**First Week Goals:**
1. Import or log recent hunt data to see analytics in action
2. Set up advanced tracking for pattern analysis
3. Consider contributing to community discussions
4. Explore premium analytics features

**Respect Message:**
"Your knowledge and experience strengthen our entire community. We're here to provide tools that match your expertise."

### Expert/Professional Onboarding Path
**Welcome Message:**
"Welcome to GoHunta! We're honored to have a professional join our community and look forward to the expertise you'll share."

**Personalized Recommendations:**
- Immediate expert network access
- Content contribution opportunities
- Advanced platform features
- Community leadership opportunities

**First Week Goals:**
1. Complete professional profile verification
2. Explore expert-level features and analytics
3. Consider joining our expert contributor program
4. Connect with other professionals in the network

**Professional Recognition:**
"Your professional expertise helps elevate the entire hunting community. We're committed to providing tools that meet your professional standards."

## Progressive Disclosure Strategy

### Week 1: Core Functions
- Basic hunt logging
- Simple dog profiles (if applicable)
- Community browsing
- Mobile app basics

### Week 2: Enhanced Features
- Advanced hunt data fields
- Training session logging
- Route planning basics
- Community participation

### Week 3: Analytics and Insights
- Pattern recognition in hunt data
- Dog training progress analysis
- Success rate trending
- Advanced community features

### Week 4: Optimization and Community Leadership
- Data-driven hunting strategy development
- Premium feature exploration
- Community contribution encouragement
- Mentorship opportunities

This onboarding framework ensures new users understand GoHunta's value quickly while gradually introducing more advanced features as they become comfortable with the platform.
