# GoHunta Community Guidelines & Moderation Framework

## Community Mission Statement

GoHunta is a community built by hunters, for hunters, where ethical hunting practices, respect for wildlife, and mutual support create an environment where hunters of all experience levels can learn, grow, and succeed together.

## Core Community Values

### 1. Respect and Inclusion
**What this means:**
All hunters are welcome regardless of experience level, background, hunting style, or location. We celebrate the diversity of hunting traditions while maintaining shared ethical standards.

**In practice:**
- Welcome newcomers with patience and encouragement
- Share knowledge without condescension or gatekeeping
- Respect different hunting traditions and regional practices
- Support hunters working to improve their skills and ethics

**What we don't tolerate:**
- Discrimination based on experience, gender, age, or background
- Elitist attitudes or exclusionary behavior
- Dismissive responses to legitimate questions
- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind

### 2. Ethical Hunting Practices
**What this means:**
We promote and celebrate ethical hunting that follows fair chase principles, respects wildlife, supports conservation, and maintains hunting's positive image in society.

**In practice:**
- Share techniques that emphasize skill, patience, and fair chase
- Discuss conservation efforts and habitat stewardship
- Celebrate clean, ethical harvests and responsible hunting decisions
- Support hunters learning to make ethical choices

**What we don't tolerate:**
- Promotion of illegal hunting practices
- Disrespectful treatment of wildlife in content or discussion
- Encouraging unsafe or unethical hunting behaviors
- Content that damages hunting's public image

### 3. Safety First
**What this means:**
Safety is non-negotiable in all hunting activities. We promote and reinforce safe hunting practices, equipment handling, and decision-making.

**In practice:**
- Always emphasize safety in advice and recommendations
- Share safety tips and best practices regularly
- Support hunters learning proper safety protocols
- Acknowledge safety concerns seriously and respectfully

**What we don't tolerate:**
- Advice that compromises hunter safety
- Casual attitudes toward firearm safety or other safety protocols
- Encouraging risky behavior for any reason
- Dismissing legitimate safety concerns

### 4. Constructive Knowledge Sharing
**What this means:**
We share knowledge, experiences, and advice in ways that help others improve their hunting success while building community connections.

**In practice:**
- Share specific, actionable advice based on experience
- Ask clarifying questions to better understand problems
- Acknowledge when you don't know something and refer to experts
- Celebrate others' successes and learning progress

**What we don't tolerate:**
- Sharing unverified or potentially dangerous advice
- Making claims about techniques you haven't personally used
- Competitive behavior that discourages community learning
- Withholding important safety or ethical information

## Community Guidelines by Content Type

### Hunt Reports and Success Stories
**Encouraged Content:**
- Detailed accounts that help others learn
- Photos that show ethical hunting and respectful game handling
- Discussion of techniques, strategies, and lessons learned
- Recognition of conservation efforts and habitat stewardship

**Content Standards:**
- Photos must show respectful presentation of harvested game
- Stories should emphasize skill, ethics, and fair chase principles
- Include information that helps others improve their hunting
- Acknowledge others who contributed to hunt success (guides, landowners, etc.)

**Not Permitted:**
- Disrespectful photos or presentation of harvested animals
- Content that emphasizes quantity over quality or ethics
- Overly graphic images that could damage hunting's image
- Claims about illegal activities or unethical practices

### Training Discussions and Advice
**Encouraged Content:**
- Step-by-step training methods with clear explanations
- Problem-solving discussions for specific training challenges
- Success stories that show training progression and results
- Resources for continued learning and skill development

**Content Standards:**
- Advice must be based on actual experience or expert knowledge
- Include appropriate disclaimers for complex or advanced techniques
- Acknowledge when professional help might be needed
- Respect different training philosophies and approaches

**Not Permitted:**
- Training advice that could harm dogs or compromise their welfare
- Unverified claims about training methods or results
- Promotion of training techniques that violate animal welfare standards
- Discouraging others from seeking professional training help

### Equipment Reviews and Recommendations
**Encouraged Content:**
- Honest reviews based on actual field experience
- Specific use cases and performance details
- Comparisons that help others make informed decisions
- Budget-conscious options and value assessments

**Content Standards:**
- Clearly identify sponsored content or business relationships
- Base recommendations on actual use and performance
- Include both strengths and limitations of equipment
- Consider different needs and budgets in recommendations

**Not Permitted:**
- False or misleading claims about equipment performance
- Hidden commercial relationships or undisclosed sponsorships
- Recommendations for equipment that could compromise safety
- Attacks on other users' equipment choices or budget constraints

### Regional and Location Discussions
**Encouraged Content:**
- General area information that helps visiting hunters
- Seasonal timing and strategy discussions
- Regulations and access information updates
- Conservation and habitat improvement opportunities

**Content Standards:**
- Share information that benefits the broader hunting community
- Respect private property and access arrangements
- Include current regulation and access status
- Encourage ethical hunting practices in all locations

**Not Permitted:**
- Specific location details that could damage hunting opportunities
- Information about private property without landowner permission
- Advice that could lead to regulation violations or access problems
- Content that could result in overpressure on hunting areas

## Moderation Approach and Procedures

### Community Self-Moderation
**Encouraging Positive Behavior:**
- Community members are encouraged to model good behavior
- Gentle corrections for minor guideline violations
- Positive reinforcement for helpful contributions
- Welcoming approach to new members and questions

**When to Report Content:**
- Safety violations or potentially dangerous advice
- Disrespectful or discriminatory behavior
- Content that violates ethical hunting standards
- Spam, commercial abuse, or off-topic content

### Moderation Response Levels

#### Level 1: Gentle Guidance
**For:** Minor guideline violations, new member orientation needs
**Response:** Friendly comment or private message explaining community expectations
**Example:** "Welcome to GoHunta! Just a heads up that we like to include safety reminders with equipment advice. Thanks for sharing your experience!"

#### Level 2: Community Education
**For:** More significant violations, repeated minor issues
**Response:** Public clarification of guidelines with educational focus
**Example:** "This is a good reminder of our community's focus on ethical hunting practices. Here's how we can discuss this topic in a way that supports our shared values..."

#### Level 3: Content Moderation
**For:** Clear violations that require content removal or editing
**Response:** Content removal with explanation and guidance for future participation
**Example:** "We've removed this content because it doesn't meet our safety standards. Please review our guidelines and feel free to repost with appropriate safety information included."

#### Level 4: Account Restrictions
**For:** Repeated violations, serious safety or ethics violations
**Response:** Temporary restrictions with clear path to restoration
**Example:** "Your account has been temporarily restricted due to repeated guideline violations. Please review our community standards and contact support to discuss reinstatement."

#### Level 5: Community Removal
**For:** Severe violations, illegal content, persistent disruption
**Response:** Permanent removal from community with clear explanation
**Used only for:** Illegal activity promotion, persistent harassment, dangerous advice that cannot be corrected

## Communication Templates for Moderation

### Welcome Messages for New Members
**Standard Welcome:**
"Welcome to the GoHunta community! We're glad you're here. Take a moment to review our community guidelines to get the most out of your experience. If you have questions, don't hesitate to ask - we're all here to help each other succeed."

**New Hunter Welcome:**
"Welcome to GoHunta! It's great to see new hunters joining our community. Everyone here started where you are now, and we're excited to help you learn and grow. Our community guidelines will help you understand how we support each other. Looking forward to hearing about your hunting journey!"

### Guideline Violation Responses

**Safety Concern Response:**
"Thanks for sharing your experience! For the safety of our community, we need to address [specific safety issue]. Here's the safer approach: [correct information]. We appreciate your understanding and commitment to keeping everyone safe."

**Ethical Hunting Reminder:**
"We appreciate you sharing your hunt! Our community emphasizes [specific ethical principle]. Here's how we can celebrate your success while maintaining our ethical standards: [guidance]. Thanks for being part of a community that values responsible hunting."

**Respectful Discussion Redirect:**
"This is an important topic that hunters should discuss. Let's keep our conversation focused on [appropriate focus] so everyone can benefit from the discussion. Thanks for helping maintain our respectful community atmosphere."

### Escalation Communication

**First Warning:**
"This content/behavior doesn't align with our community guidelines. Specifically: [issue explanation]. Please review our guidelines and adjust future posts accordingly. We're here to help if you have questions about community expectations."

**Second Warning:**
"We've previously discussed community guidelines with you, and this content still doesn't meet our standards. To continue participating in our community, please [specific required actions]. Our guidelines exist to keep our community helpful and welcoming for all hunters."

**Final Warning Before Restriction:**
"This is your final warning regarding community guideline compliance. Future violations will result in account restrictions. Please take time to thoroughly review our guidelines before posting again. We want you to succeed in our community and hope you'll choose to participate respectfully going forward."

### Appeal Process Communication

**Appeal Acknowledgment:**
"We've received your appeal regarding [moderation action]. We'll review the situation and respond within 48 hours. Appeals are handled by a different team member to ensure fairness. Thank you for your patience."

**Appeal Approval:**
"After reviewing your appeal, we're reversing the moderation action. [Explanation of decision]. We appreciate your commitment to following our guidelines and welcome your continued participation in our community."

**Appeal Denial:**
"After careful review, we're maintaining the original moderation decision. [Brief explanation]. We hope you'll use this as an opportunity to better understand our community standards and participate positively in the future."
