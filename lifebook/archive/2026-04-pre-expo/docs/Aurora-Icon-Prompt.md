# Aurora App Icon - Sora/AI Image Generation Prompt

## Complete Prompt for Sora/DALL-E/Midjourney

```
Create a professional iOS app icon for "Aurora" - a memoir recording application designed for elderly users to preserve their life stories.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Dimensions: 1024x1024 pixels, perfect square
- Format: High-resolution, crisp, clean edges
- Style: Modern iOS app icon (iOS 17/18 design language)
- No text or words on the icon
- No pre-rounded corners (iOS applies them automatically)

DESIGN CONCEPT:
Primary visual: A warm, glowing microphone emerging from or surrounded by gentle aurora borealis light rays, creating a sense of voice meeting memory. The microphone should be simple, modern, and welcoming - not technical or intimidating.

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Central focus: Simplified microphone icon (warm, approachable, not studio-quality)
- Background: Soft aurora/sunrise gradient suggesting warmth, memory, and nostalgia
- Light rays or glow suggesting captured memories becoming stories
- Optional subtle elements: Very faint book pages or memory motifs in the background

COLOR PALETTE (EXACT):
- Primary: Teal #4A9B9B (cool, trustworthy, calming)
- Secondary: Warm beige #F5E6D3 (nostalgic, gentle, accessible)
- Accent: Soft sunset orange/coral #FF9B7A (warmth, life, vitality)
- Highlights: Soft white/cream for light rays
- Shadows: Deep teal or warm brown for depth

STYLE GUIDELINES:
- Design language: Modern, clean, minimalist
- Emotional tone: Warm, inviting, nostalgic, trustworthy
- Accessibility: High contrast, easily recognizable at small sizes (60x60px)
- Age-appropriate: Gentle, not trendy or edgy - timeless design
- Avoid: Sharp angles, aggressive colors, complex details, technical aesthetics

COMPOSITION:
- Centered microphone (40-50% of icon space)
- Gradient background flowing from warm (top) to cool (bottom) or vice versa
- Subtle depth through soft shadows and highlights
- Balanced negative space for clarity
- Icon should work in both light and dark mode iOS interfaces

INSPIRATION REFERENCES:
- Voice Memos app (Apple) - simplicity and clarity
- Overcast podcast app - warm, approachable audio icon
- Day One journal app - memory and storytelling aesthetic
- GoodNotes - warmth combined with professionalism

QUALITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Professional, App Store ready
- Scalable appearance (looks good from 60px to 1024px)
- Memorable and distinctive among other apps
- Evokes emotions: nostalgia, warmth, connection, preservation
- Target audience: Elderly users and their families
- Conveys: "Share your voice, preserve your memories"

OUTPUT:
Generate a single, centered, polished iOS app icon design that an elderly person would feel comfortable tapping to record their cherished life memories. The icon should feel like a warm invitation to share stories, not a technical recording tool.
```

## Icon Design Rationale

**Why these elements?**

🎙️ **Microphone:**
- Core function: Voice recording
- Universal symbol users recognize
- Simple, non-intimidating shape

🌅 **Aurora/Sunrise Gradient:**
- App name: "Aurora"
- Symbolizes: Dawn of memories, light preservation
- Emotional: Warmth, nostalgia, life's journey

📖 **Subtle Book/Pages (optional):**
- Secondary theme: Stories, memoirs
- Not primary focus (avoid clutter)
- Can be hinted at in background

🎨 **Color Psychology:**
- **Teal:** Trust, calm, clarity (medical/health apps use this)
- **Warm Beige:** Nostalgia, comfort, accessibility
- **Coral/Orange:** Vitality, warmth, human connection
